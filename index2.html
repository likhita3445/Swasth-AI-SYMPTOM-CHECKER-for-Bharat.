What is SwasthAI?

Multilingual AI Symptom Checker for rural India

SwasthAI is a smart digital health assistant that uses Artificial Intelligence to help people understand their health symptoms. Unlike many standard apps, it supports regional languages and lets users speak or type health concerns in Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, and more. The tool serves as a preliminary guide, giving a clear risk level of Low, Medium, or High so users know how quickly they should seek medical care.

The Problem: Healthcare Barriers in Rural Areas

  • 1 Limited access: Many residents must travel long distances for even a basic consultation.
  • 2 Language barriers: Most health apps are only in English, making them hard to use comfortably.
  • 3 Connectivity issues: Unreliable internet often makes online health services unusable when they are needed most.

Our Solution: Key Features

  • ✓ Voice & language support: Users can speak naturally in their local dialect, making the app easy to use even without strong literacy.
  • ✓ Intelligent risk assessment: AI analyzes symptoms and provides simple, reliable guidance.
  • ✓ Offline functionality: A local database enables guidance even when internet access is unavailable.
  • ✓ Emergency assistance: The app can locate nearby hospitals and share ambulance contact details during urgent situations.
